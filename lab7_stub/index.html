My Travel Destinations

Cold Spring, NY

My favorite place to visit is Cold Spring, NY. It's a small town on the Hudson River, about an hour north of New York City. The town is full of cute shops, great restaurants, and beautiful views of the river and surrounding mountains. I love to spend the day walking around the town, browsing the shops, and enjoying a meal at one of the local restaurants.

Some of my favorite things to do in Cold Spring is to go hiking in nearby Trails. Here are some of my Recommendations

  1. Bull Hill Hike - A moderate hike offering stunning views of the Hudson River and surrounding mountains.
  2. Breakneck Ridge Hike - A challenging hike known for its steep climbs and breathtaking panoramic views.
  3. Mount Taurus Hike - A scenic hike with beautiful forest trails and rewarding summit views.

Spiti Valley, Himachal Pradesh, India

Spiti Valley is a cold desert mountain valley located high in the Himalayas in the north-eastern part of the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. The name "Spiti" means "The Middle Land", i.e. the land between Tibet and India. The valley is one of the least populated regions in India and is known for its stunning landscapes, ancient monasteries, and unique culture.

Some of my favorite places to visit in Spiti Valley are:

  1. Kaza - The main town in Spiti Valley and a great place to base yourself for exploring the region.
  2. Key Monastery - A beautiful Tibetan Buddhist monastery located on a hilltop overlooking the Spiti River.
  3. Pin Valley National Park - A beautiful national park known for its stunning landscapes, diverse flora and fauna, and opportunities for trekking and wildlife spotting.
