Travel Tips and Tricks

  1. Decide on Mode of Transport

    Before you start planning your trip, decide on the mode of transport you will be using. Whether you are flying, driving, or taking public transportation, this will help you plan your itinerary and budget accordingly.

  2. Research Your Destination

    Research your destination before you go. Learn about the local culture, customs, and language. This will help you navigate the area more easily.

  3. Download offline maps

    Download offline maps of the area you will be visiting especially if you are going hiking. This will help you navigate the area even if you don't have access to the internet.

  4. Pack Light

    When packing for your trip, remember to pack light. Only bring the essentials and leave room for souvenirs and other items you may pick up along the way.

  5. Carry necessary equipments

    Carry necessary equipments like power banks, insect repellent sprays, medicines. This will help you stay connected and be safe throughout your trip.
