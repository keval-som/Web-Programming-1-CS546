Day hike near NYC

11:30 PM : 11/02/2024

Last week, feeling frustrated from studies, I took a break and went hiking to Cold Spring. The weather was perfect, with clear skies and a gentle breeze. The trail was challenging but rewarding, offering stunning views of the Hudson River and the surrounding mountains. Along the way, I encountered various wildlife, including deer and a variety of birds. It was a refreshing and rejuvenating experience that helped me clear my mind and return to my studies with renewed energy.

Cold Desert Paradise

11:45 PM : 11/02/2024

Last year, I had the opportunity to visit Spiti Valley in Himachal Pradesh, India. The valley is a cold desert mountain valley located high in the Himalayas and is one of the least populated regions in India. The landscape is stunning, with snow-capped mountains, crystal-clear rivers, and ancient monasteries dotting the landscape. I spent my days exploring the region, visiting monasteries, and trekking through the beautiful valleys. The people were warm and welcoming, and the culture was fascinating. It was a truly unforgettable experience that I will cherish forever.

Location

Spiti Valley, HP, India
